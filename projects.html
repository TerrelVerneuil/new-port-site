Anki Cozmo Localization

By using the Anki Cozmo robotics platform, We will simulate the Kidnapped Robot Problem, using an implementation of Monte Carlo Localization. By using the Anki Cozmo Robot it should figure out where it is, and based on the culmination of its rotation and location data, the robot should be able to familiarize itself with its environment, and figure out where it is after being “Kidnapped”.

Mountain Car Problem

The Mountain Car problem is a classic reinforcement learning challenge that involves an underpowered car needing to reach the top of a steep hill. The vehicle is located in a valley and must utilize potential energy from the opposite hill to gain enough momentum to reach the goal. This problem exemplifies decision-making under uncertainty and learning through environmental interaction.

Languages Used:

  • Python
  • Java
